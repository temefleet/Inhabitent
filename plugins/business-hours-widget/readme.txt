This plugin adds a custom field to easily input business hours in the Inhabitent Theme WordPress interface.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How are the hours divided? =

They're divided into 3 sections Monday to Friday, Saturday and Sunday.

= What about the hours in the footer? =

That functionality is not available at this time.
